Harvest Seasonality Calendar

Pasture farming cycles fluctuate naturally with the solar seasons. Review our peak seasonal availability chart:

🌸 Spring

Peak Egg Production

Hens lay at full volume as vegetation blooms. Fresh spring wildflower pollens enrich egg yolk profiles.

☀️ Summer

Peak Poultry & Duck Eggs

Broilers forage warm green clover fields. Peak season for large pasture duck eggs and honey.

🍁 Autumn

Heritage Turkeys & Hives

Final combs honey harvest. Flocks supplement grazing with organic winter grains and root feeds.

❄️ Winter

Fresh Broths & Stock Packs

Flocks retreat to dry mobile hoop shelters. High demand for bone broths and slow-cook cuts.
