Our Pasture Welfare Pledge

We treat our birds with the utmost respect. Here is how our pasture-raised welfare standards translate to healthy flocks and premium eggs.

Rotational Pastures

Weekly field shifts prevent soil degradation, providing our flocks with clean soil and fresh clover forage cycles.

GMO-Free Grain

Hens supplement their grass diets with high-grade local grains, completely free from artificial growth hormones or medications.

Guardian Dogs

Our pastures are guarded 24/7 by trained Maremma sheepdogs, protecting our flocks from hawks and local predators naturally.

Coop Arches

Mobile shelters provide warm nesting boxes, clean wood shavings, and dry refuge from seasonal wind and rain.
