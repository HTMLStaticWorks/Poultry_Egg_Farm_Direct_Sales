Safety Guidelines

To keep our visitors and chickens safe, we strictly enforce these bio-security and safety regulations:

  • ⚠️
    Footwear: Closed-toe shoes (preferably boots) are mandatory. Sandals or heels are not permitted on pasture pathways.
  • ⚠️
    Sanitization: Foot-wash stations are placed at coop entry points to protect our flocks from outside avian diseases.
  • ⚠️
    Animal Interaction: Chickens can be held under farmer supervision. Running after or chasing hens is strictly forbidden.
  • ⚠️
    Weather: Tours operate light-rain or shine. Heavy storms will reschedule bookings.

Farm Tour Attractions

Brooder Nest

Meet day-old baby chicks under warm lights, learn about embryology and feather development.

🐓

Pasture Arches

Walk inside rotated fields, watch the hens scratch for clover, and try hand-feeding our flocks.

Solar Wash Station

See how eggs are gently cleaned in warm rainwater and graded under inspectors' crack scanners.

🐝

Apiary Meadow

Safely view our honeybee hives and see wildflower honeycombs being hand-centrifuged.
