Direct Delivery & Logistics

To guarantee freshness, we operate our own refrigerated delivery fleet instead of using third-party transit services. We handle the load from washing room to receiving bays.

  • 🚚
    Temperature Tracked: Fleet maintains strict USDA cold storage temperature guidelines (under 45°F).
  • 🚚
    Regular Schedules: Set delivery routes cover North, East, and South metropolitan sectors twice weekly.
  • 🚚
    Minimum Order: Free delivery on wholesale orders exceeding $150. A modest $25 fuel surcharge is applied below threshold.

Wholesale Quality Compliance

We supply bulk orders with absolute transparency. Our washing, butchery, and shipping systems undergo rigorous safety checks:

HAACP Safety Protocols

Our washing and cutting facilities operate under standard Hazard Analysis Critical Control Point guidelines to ensure pathogen control.

Cold Chain Verification

Every shipment is packed in temperature-controlled boxes with active cold logging, guaranteeing delivery below 42°F (5.5°C).

Third-Party Inspections

Our pastures and flocks undergo annual independent audits to maintain Humane Handling and Certified Bio-Safety ranks.
